Aims & Scope

Defining the Frontier of Interdisciplinary Applied Research

Our Mission

The SOLAV Journal (Scholarly Open-Library of Applied Visioneering) aims to be the premier open-access platform for high-impact research that transcends traditional disciplinary boundaries. We are dedicated to publishing work that not only advances fundamental understanding but explicitly directs that knowledge toward tangible applications, innovative solutions, and forward-looking "visioneering" for societal and technological benefit.

Our continuous publication model ensures that these pioneering ideas are disseminated to the global research community and industry practitioners with minimal delay, fostering immediate collaboration and accelerating the path from discovery to real-world implementation.

Core Aims of the Journal

SOLAV is founded on the following key principles and objectives:

Bridge Disciplines

Actively foster and highlight research at the intersection of established fields (e.g., bio-engineering, computational social science, sustainable materials chemistry) where breakthrough innovations occur.

Accelerate Application

Prioritize studies with clear pathways to application, technology transfer, or policy impact. We value research that addresses concrete challenges and proposes viable solutions.

Champion Open Science

Remove all access barriers by publishing under a permissive CC BY license. We support open methodologies, data, and code to ensure reproducibility and maximize collective progress.

Ensure Rigor & Speed

Maintain the highest standards of peer review while leveraging a continuous publication model to reduce submission-to-publication time dramatically, giving research timely relevance.

Build a Community

Create a dynamic intellectual hub for researchers, engineers, and practitioners committed to applied problem-solving, facilitating dialogue and collaboration through our published content.

Practice Visioneering

Encourage forward-looking research that anticipates future technological needs and societal challenges, shaping the trajectory of applied science rather than merely following it.

Interdisciplinary & Transdisciplinary Focus: SOLAV prioritizes research that connects multiple domains and bridges the gap between technical innovation and societal impact. We particularly welcome submissions that demonstrate:
  • Technology-Society Interfaces: AI ethics, digital policy, sociotechnical systems
  • Human-Centered Innovation: HCI, participatory design, technology acceptance
  • Applied Social Sciences: Computational social science, behavioral economics of tech
  • Humanities-Informed Tech: Digital humanities, ethics by design, historical perspectives

Note: This list represents our core areas of interest but is not exhaustive. We encourage authors working on novel applications at the confluence of these or other fields to contact the editorial office if they are uncertain about the fit of their manuscript. SOLAV particularly values interdisciplinary research that bridges technical, social, and humanistic dimensions to address real-world challenges.

Types of Contributions

SOLAV publishes the following categories of articles:

Article Type Description Max. Words*
Research Article Original reports of significant research findings, presenting new data and analysis. 8,000
Review Article Comprehensive, critical surveys of existing literature and state-of-the-art in a particular field. 10,000
Short Communication Brief reports of urgent, preliminary, or highly significant findings requiring rapid dissemination. 3,000
Case Study Detailed analysis of a specific real-world application, implementation, or project. 5,000
Perspective / Vision Forward-looking articles presenting new viewpoints, hypotheses, or speculative "visioneering" on future directions. 4,000
Methodology / Protocol Detailed descriptions of novel experimental or computational methods, tools, or protocols. 6,000

*Word limits are inclusive of abstract, main text, references, and figure/table legends. Please consult the complete Author Guidelines for detailed formatting instructions.
