Accessibility Statement

Last updated: 2026

Our Commitment

SOLAV Journal is committed to making its website accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We strive to meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards and continuously improve accessibility.

Accessibility Features

Our website includes:

  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Screen reader compatibility
  • Alternative text for images
  • Clear heading structure
  • Resizable text without loss of functionality
  • High contrast color options

PDF Accessibility

We encourage authors to provide accessible PDFs with proper tagging, reading order, and alternative text. Our editorial team provides guidance on creating accessible documents.

Known Limitations

Some older PDFs may not be fully accessible. We are working to improve this. If you encounter accessibility barriers, please contact us for alternative formats.

Third-Party Content

Some content (e.g., embedded videos, interactive elements) may come from third parties with varying accessibility levels. We encourage providers to maintain accessibility standards.

Feedback & Assistance

We welcome feedback on accessibility. If you encounter barriers or need assistance:

  • Email: accessibility@solav.me
  • Response time: Within 5 business days
  • We will provide accessible alternatives upon request

Continuous Improvement

We regularly review and test our website for accessibility. Our development team receives accessibility training, and we incorporate accessibility into all website updates.

Compliance Standards

We aim to comply with:

  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 AA
  • Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act
  • Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act (AODA)
  • EN 301 549 European standard
Our Promise

We believe scholarly knowledge should be accessible to everyone. We are committed to removing barriers and providing equal access to our published research.

SOLAV Journal is an open access, peer-reviewed journal publishing interdisciplinary applied research with real-world impact.
Publication Frequency: Continuous publication.
