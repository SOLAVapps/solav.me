SOLAV Journal is a diamond open access publication with no subscription fees for readers. All articles are freely available immediately upon publication under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) license, maximizing dissemination and reuse of interdisciplinary applied research.

Key Open Access Benefits

• No reader fees - Anyone can read, download, copy, and share
• Maximum visibility - Articles accessible worldwide
• Higher citation impact - Open access articles cited more frequently
• Public benefit - Taxpayer-funded research available to all

Copyright & Author Rights

Author Copyright

Authors retain copyright of their published work while granting SOLAV Journal certain publishing rights:

  • Authors retain full copyright ownership
  • Authors grant SOLAV first publication rights
  • Authors can deposit in repositories immediately
  • Authors can reuse in future works without restriction
  • No transfer of copyright to the journal
Journal Publishing Rights

SOLAV Journal receives the following non-exclusive rights:

  • Right to publish and distribute the article
  • Right to translate into different languages
  • Right to create adaptations and summaries
  • Right to include in databases and indexes
  • Right to sublicense under same CC BY terms
Copyright Agreement

Upon manuscript acceptance, authors complete a Publishing Agreement that formalizes these rights. The agreement does not transfer copyright but grants necessary publishing permissions. Authors receive a copy for their records.

Self-Archiving & Repository Policy

We encourage authors to self-archive their work to increase visibility and ensure long-term preservation. Our policy follows the green open access model with immediate deposit rights.

Version Can Be Archived When Requirements
Submitted Version
(Pre-peer review)		 Yes Immediately Must cite final published version when available
Accepted Version
(Post-peer review, pre-typesetting)		 Yes Immediately Include DOI/citation to published article
Published Version
(Final typeset PDF)		 Yes Immediately Must include proper attribution and CC BY notice
Recommended Repositories
  • Institutional repositories - University/library systems
  • Disciplinary repositories - arXiv, bioRxiv, SSRN
  • ResearchGate/Academia.edu - Professional networks
  • Personal websites - Author or lab websites
Social Media Sharing
  • Full articles can be shared on any platform
  • No embargo on sharing published versions
  • Preprints can be promoted before publication
  • Altmetrics tracking encouraged

Reuse Permissions & Citations

How to Cite SOLAV Articles

When reusing SOLAV content, provide attribution in this format:

Author(s). Volume(Year). Article Title. SOLAV Journal, Page range. https://solav.me/a/XXXXX

Example: Smith, J., & Lee, A. 1(2025). Applied interdisciplinary approaches to sustainable energy. SOLAV Journal, 45-67. https://solav.me/a/s202578XXXX

Permissions for Reusers

No permission required for:

  • Classroom use and course packs
  • Conference presentations
  • Institutional repositories
  • Translation into other languages
  • Inclusion in books or anthologies

Simply provide proper attribution as specified in the CC BY license.

Third-Party Material

Authors are responsible for obtaining permission to reproduce any third-party material (images, tables, etc.) in their articles. Such material should be clearly identified with proper attribution. SOLAV Journal cannot publish material without appropriate permissions.
