Welcome to SOLAV

SOLAV (A Scholarly Platform for Interdisciplinary Applied Futures Research) is a rigorously peer-reviewed, open-access journal dedicated to the rapid dissemination of significant research across a broad spectrum of applied sciences and interdisciplinary technology. We operate on a continuous publication model, ensuring your work is published as soon as the review process is complete.

Advancing Scholarship Through Open Collaboration

In an era of accelerating discovery and global challenges, SOLAV serves as a scholarly commons where rigorous research meets broad accessibility. We believe knowledge should be a public good, not a private commodity.

Global Scholarly Commons

We transcend geographic and institutional boundaries, creating an inclusive platform where researchers from universities, independent laboratories, industry R&D, and grassroots innovation communities contribute equally. Academic merit, not affiliation, defines contribution.

Accelerated Knowledge Cycle

Our continuous publication model eliminates artificial delays, ensuring that significant findings reach the scholarly community without unnecessary waiting. From final acceptance to citable publication typically occurs within 5-7 business days, accelerating the research lifecycle.

Rigor with Purpose

We maintain uncompromising scholarly standards through rigorous peer review while embracing innovative methodologies and interdisciplinary approaches. Quality assessment is constructive, transparent, and focused on advancing understanding rather than gatekeeping.

Inclusive Excellence

SOLAV active pe-autoly welcomes contributions from early-career researchers, independent scholars, practitioners, and established academics alike. We evaluate submissions based on intellectual contribution, methodological rigor, and relevance, not institutional pedigree or geography.

A Platform for All Research Communities

Academic Researchers

For university-affiliated scholars, SOLAV offers:

  • Rigorous peer review meeting academic tenure requirements
  • Rapid dissemination supporting grant reporting and impact metrics
  • Interdisciplinary visibility beyond traditional departmental boundaries
  • Compliance with open access mandates from funders and institutions
  • Permanent archiving for long-term citation stability

Independent Scholars & Practitioners

For researchers outside traditional academia, SOLAV provides:

  • Merit-based evaluation without institutional bias
  • Global visibility for innovative work from industry, NGOs, and independent labs
  • Practical impact pathways for applied research and technical solutions
  • Support for mixed-methods research bridging theory and practice
  • Collaborative opportunities across academic-practice boundaries

Bridging Communities

SOLAV deliberately creates space for dialogue between academic research and applied practice. We value contributions that translate theoretical insights into practical solutions, and that ground technical innovations in scholarly understanding. This exchange enriches both communities and advances knowledge with real-world relevance.

Open Submission Policy: SOLAV evaluates all manuscripts based on scholarly merit, originality, and relevance. We welcome submissions from researchers at academic institutions, independent scholars, practitioners, and research teams from diverse geographic and institutional backgrounds.

Why Choose SOLAV for Your Research?

Our scholarly infrastructure is designed to maximize the impact and reach of your work while maintaining rigorous academic standards.

Rapid Publication Cycle

Our continuous publication model eliminates issue-based delays. From final acceptance to permanent, citable online publication typically occurs within 5-7 business days, ensuring timely dissemination of significant findings. Articles are archived for permanent access.

Unrestricted Global Access

All research is freely available worldwide under a CC BY 4.0 license, maximizing readership, citation potential, and societal impact. We remove financial barriers to ensure knowledge reaches researchers, practitioners, policymakers, and the public globally.

Rigorous Scholarly Assessment

Every submission undergoes thorough, Single-blind peer review by at least two domain experts. Our editorial board ensures constructive, timely evaluation that upholds academic standards while providing developmental feedback to strengthen scholarship.

Interdisciplinary Engagement

SOLAV active pe-autoly cultivates scholarship at disciplinary intersections. We provide dedicated space for work bridging engineering with life sciences, data science with social impact, or fundamental research with applied technology, where transformative insights emerge.

Enhanced Research Visibility

  • Discovery through international research infrastructures
  • OAI-PMH compliance for repository harvesting
  • Full-text indexing by major search engines

Long-term Preservation

  • Permanent ID and landing page
  • Multiple redundant backups and version control
  • Permanent preservation via Zenodo (CERN), a CoreTrustSeal-certified repository.

Scholarly Community Building

By publishing with SOLAV, you join a growing international community of researchers committed to open science, interdisciplinary dialogue, and accelerating the translation of discovery into impact. Your work contributes to a scholarly commons that values diversity of perspective, methodological innovation, and practical relevance.

The Scholarly Publication Journey

From manuscript submission to global dissemination, our process prioritizes academic rigor, author support, and timely communication.

1

Submission & Initial Screening

Authors submit via our online system. Editorial staff conduct initial checks for scope, formatting, and ethical compliance, with communication within 3 business days.

2

Expert Peer Review

The manuscript is assigned to an academic editor and sent for in-depth evaluation by independent experts selected for methodological and domain expertise.

3

Editorial Decision & Revision

Based on comprehensive reviewer feedback, the editor makes a decision. Authors receive constructive guidance for revision when appropriate.

4

Publication & Global Dissemination

Upon final acceptance, the article undergoes professional formatting and is published online within days, with immediate global accessibility and promotion.
