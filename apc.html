SOLAV Journal is committed to diamond open access publishing with transparent Article Processing Charges (APC) that support sustainable operations. Our fees cover essential publishing services while maintaining accessibility through comprehensive waiver programs.

Fee Transparency

All APC revenues directly fund journal operations: platform maintenance, editorial management, production, and long-term digital preservation. No fees are collected until manuscript acceptance.

APC Structure & Details

Standard APC

$550

Flat fee per accepted article, regardless of length or format.

Payment Timing

Due after acceptance and before final publication. No submission fees.

Payment Method

Currently accepting PayPal only. Invoice available upon request for institutional payments.

What APC Covers

  • Rigorous peer review management
  • Professional copyediting and typesetting
  • Plagiarism checking software
  • Online hosting and server maintenance
  • Digital preservation and archiving
  • Editorial board support
  • Platform development and security
  • Long-term accessibility

Waiver & Discount Policy

We believe financial constraints should not hinder quality research dissemination. SOLAV offers comprehensive waiver options:

100% APC waiver for corresponding authors from:

  • Low-income economies (World Bank classification)
  • Lower-middle-income economies with HDI below 0.65
  • Countries with active armed conflicts or humanitarian crises

Automatic approval upon verification of institutional affiliation.

Partial or full waiver considered on case-by-case basis for:

  • Independent researchers without institutional funding
  • Graduate students (Master's or PhD candidates)
  • Early-career researchers within 5 years of terminal degree
  • Authors experiencing unexpected financial hardship
  • Non-profit organizations and community-based research groups

Requires brief statement of need with submission.

Bulk publication discounts available for:

  • Universities publishing 5+ articles annually: 15% discount
  • Research institutes with 10+ articles annually: 25% discount
  • Consortia agreements for multiple institutions

Contact institutional@solav.me for partnership inquiries.

Waiver Application Process
  1. Indicate waiver request during manuscript submission
  2. Provide brief justification and supporting information
  3. Await confirmation from editorial office (within 5 working days)
  4. If approved, proceed with publication at reduced or zero APC

Note: Waiver decisions are based solely on financial need and do not affect editorial evaluation.

Payment Process

Standard Payment Flow
  1. Manuscript submitted and undergoes peer review
  2. Upon acceptance, APC invoice generated
  3. Corresponding author receives payment link via PayPal
  4. Payment completed within 14 days of acceptance
  5. Upon payment confirmation, article moves to production
  6. Final version published online
Important Notes
  • No refunds after payment processing begins
  • Articles remain unpublished until APC is settled
  • Extensions available upon request (max 30 days)
  • Institutional payments may require purchase order
  • Currency: USD only (PayPal handles conversion)
Financial Transparency

SOLAV Journal operates as a non-profit academic initiative. All APC revenues are reinvested into journal operations: platform hosting (20%), editorial services (35%), production costs (15%), and technology development (30%).
