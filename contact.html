Contact SOLAV Journal

Direct access to editorial, support, and administrative teams

We are committed to responsive communication with our authors, reviewers, and readers. Please select the appropriate contact channel below to ensure your inquiry is routed to the correct team for prompt assistance.

Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 5-7 business days. For urgent matters regarding active submissions, please include your manuscript ID in the subject line.

Before You Contact Us

Many common questions are answered in our Frequently Asked Questions section. Checking the FAQ first ensures you receive immediate answers without waiting for email correspondence.

View Frequently Asked Questions

Contact channels

Editorial & Submissions

For inquiries regarding manuscript status, peer review, editorial policies, or submission guidelines.

  • General Editorial Questions

    Manuscript status inquiries, scope clarification, reviewer applications, and editorial policy questions.

    editorial@solav.me
  • Submission Technical Issues

    Problems with the online submission form, file uploads, or confirmation emails.

    support@solav.me
  • Appeals & Decisions

    Formal appeals regarding editorial decisions or rejections.

    editorial@solav.me
APC & Payments

For inquiries regarding Article Processing Charges, waivers, invoices, and payment confirmation.

  • APC Waivers & Discounts

    Requests for fee waivers, discounts, or eligibility clarification.

    apc@solav.me
  • Payment Issues

    Invoice requests, payment confirmation, or transaction errors.

    apc@solav.me
  • Institutional Partnerships

    Inquiries regarding bulk discounts or institutional agreements.

    apc@solav.me
Ethics & Integrity

For reporting misconduct, ethical concerns, conflicts of interest, or plagiarism allegations.

  • Research Misconduct

    Reporting plagiarism, data fabrication, or ethical violations (confidential).

    ethics@solav.me
  • Conflicts of Interest

    Disclosure questions or reporting undeclared conflicts.

    ethics@solav.me
  • Complaints & Appeals

    Formal complaints regarding editorial conduct or process.

    ethics@solav.me

    Requests for alternative formats or accessibility accommodations.
