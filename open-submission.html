Author Guidelines Submit Manuscript Submission Questions

SOLAV Journal maintains an open submission policy and welcomes manuscript submissions from researchers across all backgrounds, disciplines, and career stages. Authors may be affiliated with academic or research institutions, or may contribute as independent scholars, early-career researchers, or practitioners working outside traditional academic environments.

Our Commitment

The journal is committed to fostering an inclusive scholarly environment in which innovative ideas, rigorous inquiry, and diverse perspectives can be presented and critically examined. Institutional affiliation is not a criterion for editorial consideration.

Editorial decisions are made independently by the academic editorial board. The publisher (Leothar Company) has no influence over acceptance/rejection decisions.

All submissions are assessed exclusively on academic merit, methodological rigor, originality, and relevance to the journal’s scope. We believe that meaningful contributions to knowledge can emerge from anywhere within the global research community.

Key Principles

Inclusivity

The journal encourages submissions from both established and emerging researchers, including contributors from underrepresented regions, interdisciplinary fields, and non-traditional academic pathways. SOLAV Journal values intellectual diversity as a driver of scholarly advancement.

Scholarly Rigor

While embracing diversity in authorship and perspective, the journal upholds strict standards of academic quality and research integrity. All submissions must demonstrate clear research objectives, sound methodology, coherent analysis, and evidence-based conclusions.

Forward-Looking Scholarship

The journal prioritizes research that advances understanding and offers meaningful insight into contemporary scholarly, scientific, or societal challenges.

Who Can Submit?

Affiliated Researchers

Academic faculty, postdoctoral researchers, and graduate students affiliated with universities or research institutions.

Independent Scholars

Researchers without institutional affiliation who conduct rigorous, self-directed scholarly inquiry.

Practitioners & Professionals

Individuals working in industry, government, NGOs, or other applied settings who contribute evidence-based research.

Global Contributors

Researchers from all geographic regions, including those from low- and middle-income countries.

Ensuring Your Submission is Considered

To ensure your manuscript receives full consideration, please adhere to the following guidelines:

  1. Follow Author Guidelines: Ensure your manuscript complies with our formatting, structure, and referencing requirements.
  2. Demonstrate Rigor: Clearly articulate research questions, methodology, analysis, and conclusions.
  3. Highlight Contribution: Explain how your work advances knowledge or practice within or across disciplines.
  4. Disclose Conflicts: Transparently report any funding, affiliations, or potential conflicts of interest.
  5. Submit Complete Materials: Include all required elements: abstract, keywords, figures, tables, and supplementary files if applicable.

Ready to Submit?

We invite you to contribute to our scholarly community. Whether you are affiliated with an institution or pursuing independent research, your rigorous and innovative work is welcome.
