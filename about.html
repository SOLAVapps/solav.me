Our Mission

To bridge disciplinary boundaries and accelerate the translation of research into practical solutions through open, rigorous, and ethical scholarly communication.

Innovation Through Integration

We champion research that connects disciplines, creating novel solutions to complex real-world challenges.

Open Science Commitment

All research freely accessible worldwide under Creative Commons licensing, removing barriers to knowledge.

Ethical Excellence

Rigorous peer review and adherence to COPE guidelines ensure research integrity and credibility.

SOLAV adheres to the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE) Core Practices.

Global Impact

Supporting researchers worldwide with equitable publishing opportunities and comprehensive waiver policies.

Why Publish With SOLAV?

A publishing experience designed for interdisciplinary researchers

Fast & Fair Review

Single-blind peer review with 4-6 week median decision time. Constructive feedback from interdisciplinary experts.

Maximum Visibility

Open access with no reader fees. Articles optimized for search engines and academic databases.

Rigorous Standards

COPE-aligned ethics, transparent APC model, and permanent archiving for long-term accessibility.

Supportive Community

Join a growing network of interdisciplinary scholars committed to applied research with real-world impact.
