Submit Your Manuscript

Follow the steps below to submit your research for peer review

Submission Process Overview

Submitting your work to SOLAV Journal is a straightforward process designed to be efficient for authors while ensuring all necessary information is collected for a rigorous editorial review. This page will guide you through preparing your manuscript and using our online submission form.

Please read the following instructions carefully. Submissions that do not adhere to the Author Guidelines or are incomplete may experience delays.

Before You Submit: Preparation Checklist

Ensure your manuscript meets all requirements for submission.

1. Manuscript

Formatted according to our guidelines. Includes title, abstract, keywords, main text, references, figures with captions, and tables.

2. Author Info

Full names, affiliations (if any), and email addresses for all authors. Identify the corresponding author clearly.

3. File Hosting

Your manuscript (Word .docx file) and supplementary files must be uploaded to a cloud service (e.g., Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive) with link-sharing enabled.

4. Ethics & Scope

Confirm your work is original, not under review elsewhere, aligns with our scope, and adheres to ethical standards in our policies.

Online Submission Form

Once your manuscript is prepared, use the button below to access our official submission form hosted on Google Forms.

Important Notes for the Form
  • All fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required.
  • You will need the shareable link to your manuscript file (e.g., from Google Drive).
  • You can save your progress and return to the form later if needed.
  • After submission, the corresponding author will receive a confirmation email.
  1. Click the "Open Submission Form" button
    It will open in a new tab.
  2. Fill in all author and manuscript details
    Provide accurate email addresses for all correspondence.
  3. Paste your manuscript file link
    Ensure the link is set to "Anyone with the link can view."
  4. Review and submit
    You will receive a confirmation and a copy of your responses via email.
Open Submission Form

You will be redirected to Google Forms, a secure third-party service.

After Submission: What Happens Next?

Transparency in our editorial process.

1. Acknowledgement

The corresponding author receives an automatic acknowledgement email from Google Forms and a manual confirmation from our editorial office within 7 business days.

2. Editorial Check

Our team performs an initial screening for scope, completeness, and plagiarism. If suitable, it is assigned to an Editor-in-Charge.

3. Peer Review

The editor invites independent experts for single-blind peer review. You will be notified of the decision (Accept/Revise/Reject) along with reviewer comments.

Need Help or Have Questions?

Before contacting us, please ensure your question is not already answered in our Author Guidelines. For technical submission issues, email our editorial office.
